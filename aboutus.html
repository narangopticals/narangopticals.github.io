About us:

Established Since 1983, First & Oldest Optician Shop in district Udham Singh Nagar,Uttarakhand. We have served customers more than 60km from the establishment, to name few notable cities including Kashipur are Ramnagar, Almora, Ranikhet, Haldwani and the towns in between. We deal in all kind of eyewear, and in vast variety of raw materials such as Frames, Sunglasses, as well as Dispensing of Prescribed Spectacles.
